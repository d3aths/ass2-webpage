Return Policy & FAQ



We want you to be 100% happy with your purchases, so we aim to make any returns as easy as possible!

30 DAY RETURN GUARANTEE
You can return your item for an EXCHANGE, REFUND or STORE CREDIT


- Items must be returned in the original condition - product tags attached, original packaging, unworn, unused.
- Please keep the item clean from animal fur, stains & cigarette smoke. We reserve the right to reject any items that are returned in unsaleable condition.
- The customer is responsible for the return postage costs, unless there was an error with the order. In this case we will cover all costs to ensure you are not out of pocket. Exchange Postage costs - $7.95 Regular Post or $9.95 Express Post.
- All returns will be processed within 2 days of arriving back to the warehouse [there may be some slight delays on processing returns during busy times]
- Please note : Items purchased from our Sale section can be returned for a store credit only. This does NOT include items that were purchased during a sale event, eg. 15% OFF Sale, these can be returned for a full refund as normal.

FAULTY ITEMS : If you have received a faulty item we are terribly sorry! Please do not submit it through our Returns process, but instead email us directly at: orders@shop.co.nz with a photo of the item so we can arrange a solution for you right away!

PAYPAL REFUNDED RETURNS : If you paid for your order with Paypal, you can receive a refund on the return postage costs through your Paypal account.
Simply activate Refunded Returns on your PayPal account & follow the instructions in your PayPal account.
We hold customer service very highly and want all our customers to walk away from their experience happy with the service they have been provided and loving their new purchases! If you have any questions about a product or the return process, please contact us as we are happy to help in any way we can!
